# Code of Conduct

This project adheres to the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) Code of Conduct version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team at conduct@vauban.tech.
